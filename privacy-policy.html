Thank you for using Checkwise ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy notice, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at [Your Contact Email].

This privacy policy describes how we might use your information if you use our mobile application, "Checkwise". It explains what information is collected, how it is used, and what rights you have in relation to it.

Checkwise is designed to be used without creating an account. We do not require you to provide any personally identifiable information. All of your checklist data is stored locally on your device. You have the option to back up and sync your data to your own Google Drive account, but this data is never stored on our servers and is not accessible by us.

1. What Information Is Collected?

While we do not collect any personal information you provide, certain non-personal information is automatically collected by our third-party service providers when you use our app.

Information Automatically Collected

We automatically collect certain information when you use or navigate the App. This information does not reveal your specific identity (like your name or contact information) but may include device and usage information, such as:

  • Usage Data: We collect aggregated information about how you interact with our app. This includes data such as features you use, the time, frequency, and duration of your activities, and other performance data.
  • Device Data: We collect data about your mobile device, such as your device's operating system, model, and a unique device identifier.
  • Crash Reports and Analytics: We collect data about app crashes and performance to help us identify and fix bugs.

2. How Is Your Information Used?

We use the limited, anonymous information collected for the following purposes:

  • To improve our App: We use usage and behavior data to understand how our users interact with Checkwise, which helps us to improve the user experience and develop new features.
  • To ensure security and stability: Crash reports and performance data help us keep our app running smoothly and securely.

3. Third-Party Services & Data Storage

Our app integrates with third-party services for analytics and optional data backup. We do not store a copy of any data handled by these services.

  • Firebase: We use Firebase for performance monitoring and crash reporting. You can learn more about Firebase's privacy practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Analytics for Firebase & Google Tag Manager (GTM): We use these services to collect and analyze app usage data and in-app user behavior. This helps us understand which features are most popular and how we can improve the app. This data is aggregated and does not personally identify you. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices here: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Drive (Optional Sync): You may choose to back up or sync your checklist data to your personal Google Drive account. This process requires you to grant the app permission to access a specific, app-created folder in your Google Drive. We do not have access to your Google Drive account or any other files or data you store there. The storage and security of this data are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can revoke this access at any time through your Google Account settings.

4. Data Security

We rely on the security measures of our third-party providers (Google) to protect the anonymous data they collect. As all of your personal checklist data is stored on your device, you are in control of its security. If you choose to sync your data with your Google Drive account, the security of that data is subject to the security measures of your personal Google Account.

5. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. As we do not collect personally identifiable information, we do not knowingly collect such information from children.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date and the updated version will be effective as soon as it is accessible. We encourage you to review this privacy policy frequently to be informed of how we are protecting your information.
